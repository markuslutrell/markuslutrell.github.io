About us

EssayPro is an online academic writing platform that connects talented freelance writers with students in need of their services. Unlike other writing platforms, our team is made up of native English speakers from countries such as the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Ireland, and New Zealand. Our platform is highly praised by both students and writers as both parties are very satisfied with the conditions we offer.

Students receive academic help at competitively
low prices, and freelance writers receive a great
demand for their educational services, year-round. Furthermore, EssayPro places a high emphasis on delivering quality services as we value our reputation more than anything else. To put a cherry on top of the cake, EssayPro’s price to quality ratio is unmatched in the industry. For all of your academic needs, EssayPro is the way to go!
